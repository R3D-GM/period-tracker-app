\# 🌸 Period Tracker App



A comprehensive period and cycle tracking application similar to Flo.



\## Features



\- 📊 \*\*Dashboard\*\*: View your cycle insights at a glance

\- 📝 \*\*Daily Logging\*\*: Track mood, symptoms, and period flow

\- 📅 \*\*Calendar View\*\*: Visualize your cycle phases

\- 📈 \*\*Analytics\*\*: View trends and patterns in your cycle

\- 🤕 \*\*Symptom Tracker\*\*: Log and track symptoms over time

\- ⚙️ \*\*Customizable Settings\*\*: Adjust cycle length and notifications
